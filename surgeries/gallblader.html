Gallbladder Surgery

Gallbladder surgery is performed by a general surgeon in a hospital under general anesthesia. In select cases (e.g., an intolerance to general anesthesia), the operation may be performed under spinal anesthesia.1﻿﻿﻿ While the surgery can be scheduled in advance, in cases of severe inflammation, gallbladder surgery is performed emergently.
